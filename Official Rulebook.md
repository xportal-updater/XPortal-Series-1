# XPORTAL Series #1 Tournament Regulations

The XPORTAL Series tournament is organized by **XPortal Esports (GG Esports)**, a Kosovan company with Unique Registration Code (CUI) RKS 810874281. The company is legally represented by **Besnik Thaqi, CEO**.

XPORTAL Series is a **Tier 2 Valve Regional Standings (VRS) Ranked Counter-Strike 2 tournament** with an open LAN format. This event provides a welcoming competitive experience in the heart of Prishtina. As a VRS-ranked event, XPORTAL adheres to Valve’s rules for ranked tournaments, ensuring a fair structure for invites and qualifiers.  

This rulebook outlines all regulations, format details, and expectations for teams participating.

---

## Event Overview

- **Event Name:** XPORTAL Series #1 – Counter-Strike 2 Open LAN  
- **Time Zone:** All times in Prishtina local time (GMT+1)  
- **Tier & Sanctioning:** Tier 2 VRS-ranked event (counts toward Valve’s regional standings)  
- **Dates:** March 21–25, 2025  
  - March 21 (Saturday) – Media and SSD Day  
  - March 22–25 (Sunday–Wednesday) – Tournament Days  
- **Location:** TechPark Prishtina, Prishtinë, Kosovo  

---

## Number of Teams

- Maximum **16 teams**.  
- Teams consist of **5 players + 1 optional coach**.  
- **All participants must attend on-site**.

---

- Register via [www.xpseries.com](https://www.xpseries.com)  
- **Fee:** €600 per team (5 players + 1 coach)  
- Registration closes automatically on **March 5th, 23:59** or when 16 teams sign up.

## Prize Pool

- Fully funded by team entry fees: €600 per team.  
- **16 Teams → 9,600€ total prize pool**

## Registration & Eligibility

- Open to all teams globally (Valve rules apply).  
- No prior qualification required.  
- Players must be **16+** (under 18 may require parental consent).  
- All players must have valid CS2 accounts in good standing.

- Register on the official website.  
- Pay €600 (+VAT) per team within **12 hours** of registration.  
- Complete the registration form via XPORTAL Series Discord.

## Team and Roster Rules

- Up to 5 players + 1 coach per team.  
- **Roster locked 14 days before the event**.  
- Substitutions during tournament are not allowed.  
- Teams must retain **3 out of 5 core players** after roster lock.

- Based on Valve Regional Standings (VRS) released **March 2nd, 2026**.

## On-site Conduct

- Teams must follow all **venue rules** and tournament policies.

- **Stage 1:** 4 groups of 4 teams (top 2 advance).  
- **Format:** Double-elimination (GSL).

  - Remaining rounds: **BO3**

## Equipment

- Players must bring **keyboard, mouse, mousepad, in-ear headphones**.  
- Missing equipment will be provided if unavailable within 15 minutes.

---

## Tournament Format

### Group Stage (Day 1–2)

- 16 teams divided into **4 groups of 4** via seeded draw.  
- **Pot Allocation** (based on VRS rank, HLTV rank, competitive results):  
  - Pot 1: Top 4 teams  
  - Pot 2: Next 4  
  - Pot 3: Following 4  
  - Pot 4: Remaining 4  

**Group Format (GSL)**  
- Opening Matches: BO1  
- Winners Match: BO1  
- Elimination Match: BO3  
- Decider Match: BO3  

**Advancement:** Top 2 teams per group move to playoffs.

**Live Draw:** March 8, 2026 – [Twitch Channel](https://www.twitch.tv/xportalesports)

---

### Playoffs (Day 3–4)

- 8 advancing teams, **single-elimination bracket, BO3**.  
- Seeding:  
  - Match 1: Group A Winner vs Group D Runner-up  
  - Match 2: Group C Winner vs Group B Runner-up  
  - Match 3: Group D Winner vs Group A Runner-up  
  - Match 4: Group B Winner vs Group C Runner-up  

---

### Match Rules

- **Game Version:** Counter-Strike 2 (latest update by March 18)  
- **MR24** format: first to 13 rounds wins.  
- **Overtime:** MR3, $10,000 starting money.  
- **Map Pool:** Dust2, Mirage, Inferno, Nuke, Overpass, Ancient, Anubis  

**Veto Process:** Follows Valve Major standards for Bo1 and Bo3.  
**Timeouts:** 3 tactical per map, 1 extra in OT (30s each).  
**Technical Pauses:** Allowed for hardware issues.  
**Admin Pauses:** Can be invoked anytime.  

---

### Player Equipment & Setup

- Venue provides high-performance PCs and chairs.  
- Players must bring peripherals.  
- Setup time: ~5–10 minutes per match.  
- Voice comms via **local TeamSpeak**, public internet restricted.  
- Coaches can only communicate at allowed times.
